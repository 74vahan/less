Negroni
Classic 1919 · Stirred

Negroni

Ingredients

  • Gin — 30 ml
  • Campari — 30 ml
  • Sweet vermouth — 30 ml
  • Orange peel

Preparation

Stir all ingredients with ice in a mixing glass for 30 seconds. Strain into a chilled Old Fashioned glass over a large ice cube. Garnish with orange peel.

Old Fashioned
Classic 1880 · Stirred

Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • Bourbon or rye whiskey — 60 ml
  • Simple syrup — 10 ml
  • Angostura bitters — 2 dashes
  • Orange peel, cherry

Preparation

Place sugar in the glass, add bitters and a splash of water. Stir until dissolved, then add ice and whiskey. Stir gently. Express orange peel oils over the glass.

Margarita
Classic 1938 · Shaken

Margarita

Ingredients

  • Blanco tequila — 50 ml
  • Triple sec / Cointreau — 20 ml
  • Fresh lime juice — 20 ml
  • Salt for rim

Preparation

Rub the glass rim with lime and dip in coarse salt. Shake all ingredients with ice for 12–15 seconds. Double strain into a Margarita or coupe glass.

Espresso Martini
Classic 1983 · Shaken

Espresso Martini

Ingredients

  • Vodka — 40 ml
  • Kahlúa coffee liqueur — 20 ml
  • Fresh espresso — 30 ml (hot)
  • Simple syrup — 5 ml
  • 3 coffee beans for garnish

Preparation

Brew fresh espresso and let it cool slightly. Shake all ingredients vigorously with crushed ice for 15 seconds until frothy. Double strain into a Martini glass. Garnish with three beans.

Mojito
Classic 1800s · Build

Mojito

Ingredients

  • White rum — 45 ml
  • Fresh lime juice — 20 ml
  • Cane sugar syrup — 15 ml
  • Mint leaves — 8–10 pcs.
  • Soda water

Preparation

Gently muddle mint with sugar and lime in a tall glass. Add rum and crushed ice. Top with soda water. Stir gently. Garnish with mint and a lime wedge.

Dry Martini
Classic 1900s · Stirred

Dry Martini

Ingredients

  • London Dry gin — 60 ml
  • Dry vermouth — 10 ml
  • Olive or lemon twist

Preparation

Chill a Martini glass with ice. Stir gin and vermouth with ice for 45 seconds until perfectly cold. Strain into the chilled glass. Garnish with an olive or lemon twist.

My signature drink: bright, crisp, and balanced with a touch of Campari bitterness. Apple juice and gin meet lemon and pisco for a modern sour with soft citrus clarity.

02
